Yoga

Yoga:Physical Practice: Yoga is primarily a physical practice that
involves various postures (asanas), breathing techniques
(pranayama),and sometimes meditation. It aims to improve
physical health, flexibility, and strength.

Types: There are many different styles of yoga, such as Hatha,
Vinyasa, Ashtanga, and Bikram, each with its own emphasis
and techniques.
Holistic Approach: Yoga also includes ethical principles, dietary guidelines,
and a focus on the connection between mind, body, and spirit.

Meditation

Meditation:Mental Practice: Meditation is primarily a mental practice that involves
focusing the mind, often to achieve a state of relaxation, clarity, and
emotional balance.
Techniques: There are various meditation techniques, such as mindfulness,
concentration, loving-kindness, and transcendental meditation,
each with different methods of practice.
Goals: The main goals of meditation include stress reduction,
improved focus and concentration, emotional health, and
a deeper understanding of oneself.
