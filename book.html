Details Bhagavad Geeta

The Bhagavad Gita Is A Dialogue Between Arjun, A Supernaturally Gifted Warrior And His Guide And Charioteer Lord Krishna On The Battle Field Of Kurukshetra.As Both Armies Stand Ready For The Battle, The Mighty Warrior Arjun, On Observing The Warriors On Both Side Becomes Overwhelmed With Grief And Compassion Due To The Fear Of Losing His Relatives And Friends And The Consequent Sins Attributed To Killing His Own Relatives.So He Surrenders To Lord Krishna Seeking Solution. Thus Follows The Wisdom Of The Bhagavad Gita
